Welcome to Show and Tell

Show and Tell is a platform for Mines students to show off their cool projects on the ACM TV in Brown Building West.

For Students

1. Build a Cool Project

If you need inspiration, head over to the ACM TV in Brown Building West.

We also have an online showcase.

2. Submit Your Project

We support the following types of projects:

  • Terminal Applications
  • GUI Applications (Linux and Windows)
  • Websites/Web apps
  • YouTube Videos

Submit Project

3. Be Famous

We will show your project on the ACM TV in Brown Building West for everyone to see your awesome work.

People can also view your project in the web showcase interface.
